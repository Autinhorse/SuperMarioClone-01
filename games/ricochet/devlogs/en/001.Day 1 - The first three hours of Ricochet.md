# 📅 Devlog #001: Day 1 - The First 3 Hours of Ricochet

**Date: April 28, 2026**

#### 1. Today's Progress
I told myself it would be a 3-hour session, but we are well past the 4-hour mark now...

I had Claude restructure the entire repository. Now, *Ricochet* and the *Mario Maker* project are sitting side-by-side as parallel sub-projects under the `games` directory.

I drafted a `design.md` covering the absolute bare-bones mechanics and fed it to Claude. It came back with a few clarifying questions, pointed out some conflicts with the notes I wrote during the folder restructuring, and highlighted a few edge cases I hadn't even thought of. We ironed those out one by one.

And then, it spat out the very first prototype for me. <a href="../Ricochet_demo_0428_001.webm">📖 Watch the First Prototype</a> 

It’s incredibly crude, and it misses the mark on several of my original design ideas. But at the very least, it's a runnable prototype. A solid start.

#### 2. What's Next
It's currently 1:20 AM on the 29th my time. I have to be up at 6:00 AM to catch a flight, so I'm calling it a night. Committing the code and signing off.

If I can just get this prototype tweaked to actually match my requirements tomorrow, I'll be a happy man...
